Positioning Statement

Competitive Alternatives

When our target customers look to solve this problem today, they default to one of three approaches: manual research (Google searches, Reddit threads, competitor analysis spreadsheets), expensive consulting (paying $5,000-15,000 for a market research firm to produce a 30-page PDF), or building blind (skipping validation entirely and hoping the product finds a market).

Unique Attributes

  • Automated research pipeline that combines dual-LLM synthesis with real SERP validation and keyword data
  • Opinionated scoring framework (Competition, WTP, Differentiation, SEO) that produces a concrete tier recommendation rather than leaving interpretation to the user
  • Research-to-content pipeline where validated research directly feeds content generation, eliminating the handoff gap between "I know my market" and "I have a website"

Value Proposition

EPCH turns a product idea into a validated market position with a live website in hours rather than weeks. The user gets the strategic clarity of a $10K consulting engagement and the execution speed of a no-code builder, without the expense of the former or the strategic emptiness of the latter.

Target Customer

Technical founders and aspiring SaaS builders who have a product idea and want evidence-based validation before committing engineering time. They are comfortable with data, impatient with process, and would rather see a dashboard than read a slide deck.

Specifically not targeting: enterprise product teams (they have budget for Gartner), agencies (they sell the process we automate), or people without a specific product idea (our pipeline needs an input to analyze).

Market Category

Product validation for indie builders. We are creating this category rather than competing in "market research tools" (too broad, dominated by enterprise) or "landing page builders" (too narrow, misses the strategic value).

One-sentence positioning: For aspiring SaaS founders who struggle to validate product ideas before building, EPCH is a research automation platform that replaces weeks of manual market research with a one-click pipeline producing SEO analysis, competitive landscape scoring, and a live painted-door website to test real demand.
