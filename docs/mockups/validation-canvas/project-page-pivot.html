Pivot Suggestions for Reachability
5 blog posts published over 45 days showed zero organic ranking movement. The health/medical niche has high domain authority requirements that a new site can't overcome quickly through content alone. Here are alternative approaches:
1 Recommended
Target long-tail patient community keywords
"Ehlers-Danlos specialist near me" (320/mo) and "MCAS diagnosis timeline" (480/mo) have low competition from medical institutions. Patient community forums rank well — similar editorial tone.
Impact: 3 of 5 existing posts salvageable with angle shift. Painted door stays. SEO strategy doc needs regeneration.
2
Shift to Reddit + community distribution
r/undiagnosed (42k members) and r/ChronicIllness (89k members) have high engagement. Valuable content shared organically. Bypasses SEO entirely for initial traction.
Impact: Content needs reformatting for Reddit. Painted door stays. Reachability metric shifts from rankings to referral traffic.
3
Partner with existing health blogs for guest content
3 patient advocacy blogs in the rare disease space accept guest posts and have DA 40+. Backlink strategy could accelerate own domain authority while driving referral traffic.
Impact: Existing content usable as guest posts. New content needed for own site. Longer path but builds SEO foundation.
